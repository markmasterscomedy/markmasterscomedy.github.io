Comedy

 

Good clean comedy and workshops for your company, corporate event, meetup group, or at comedy showcases. I host and book the Vail Comedy Show in Vail, CO. I run a weekly online showcase called the Comedy Test Kitchen. Will travel.

Past performances out of state at the Relapse Theater in Atlanta, GA, The Misfit Hive in New York City, NY, and Silver Nugget Casino in Las Vegas, NV. (Also: AZ, CA, CO, FL, HI, IA, ID, IL, KS, MA, MO, MT, NE, NM, OR, RI, TX, UT, WA) Canada (Toronto) too!



Hometown appearances at Comedy Works in Downtown Denver, the Skyline Park Beer Garden, Voodoo Comedy Playhouse, Black Buzzard (Oskar Blues) and many other stages in Denver, CO.

Please contact me to get added to a mailing list about future appearances. Note that I am not on Facebook (or Instagram), but I respond to all website inquiries, usually the same day.
